// Toward Assembly with Justin Murphy

This week we speak with Justin Murphy, who has brought his OtherLife community and work increasingly to Urbit. We discuss Assembly, arranging marriages, leaving academia, the future of the Life of the Mind, and Justin's version of Urbit Futurism.

As we are in Asia and that is not Austin, let this be our love letter to Assembly. Justin will be making a series of high-production-value podcasts there, and as such things are done at the expense of the purse, we'd like to appeal to listeners to support Justin's endeavor.
