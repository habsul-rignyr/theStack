// Networked Subject_

This week we speak with Reid, known more commonly around Urbit as ~sitful-hatred, who runs a blog at subject.network dedicated to making communal knowledge more accessible. Whether you want to set up minio for sharing files, figure out your routing problems, or configure your bitcoin wallet, subject.network has you covered. We discuss how ~sitful-hatred got started with Urbit, the blog’s genesis, his writing for urbit.org, running a star, what he thinks Urbit will look like in the future, and more (a discussion of the recent Gurgle episode with some consideration for community norms, for instance).
