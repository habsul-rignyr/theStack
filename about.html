About The Orbis Ledger

Introduction

This is the Orbis Legder, a site for Urbit news and lifestyle. It’s operated by ~habsul-rignyr, who has written all the code without bothering to go the usual route of first being a coder. Therefore, let any success in the arousal of general interest redound to the Urbit community, whose boundless creativity and competence we herein chronicle. But if the reader finds that the CSS appears to have been written by a man trying badly to organize his morning porridge, place the blame squarely with your humble servant, H. Rignyr, Esq. As the Urbit Precepts remind us: “Not being qualified to solve a problem is no reason not to solve it.”

The problem this site is meant to solve is that its author found himself putting his nose into every corner of Urbit and had become something of an aggregator of events and information on one of those Old World social media hell sites. The Urbit Foundation has its writers, but while the Foundation creates news, it is does not have a news organ. Neither does it have an organ for the creation of long-form Urbit think-posts (though it can and has tapped its brain trust for just this, on occasion) or New Urbit City travel guides, or Urbit Review of Books, or Urbit Finance, or any other Urbit-flavored media not immediately necessary to some purpose (rather than cooked in the febrile imaginings of one Hon. H. Rignyr, Esq.).

The Current State and the Future Product

Currently, The Orbis Ledger is a static site being hosted on a VPS out of Singapore. Singapore is the place previously best known for Lee Kuan Yew and the Marina Bay Skyline. Naturally, we think The Orbis Ledger is the first thing people should think of when they think of that little island country, and encourage Urbiters to respond to any mention of it thus: “Singapore? Yes, wonderful place. Have the good luck to publish the Ledger.” Next steps in the evolution of the site are:

  • conversion to a proper PERN stack app
  • Urbit Visor integration
  • additional bells, whistles, Calm Engine Standards Compliance
  • eventual hosting on an Urbit backend

What about The三Stack Podcast?

We (~libhut-samwes and ~habsul-rignyr) will continue the podcast and it will remain free forever even if we eventually find a way to make it make money. It will become a part of the de facto Urbit News Network along with The Orbis Ledger, which is itself a step up in this impromptu hierarchy. As a token of our esteem for him, Mr. Samwes will continue to be paid by UNN twice what he is worth.
